Puerto Rico is home to 3.7 million US citizens, is part of the United States Common Wealth and is, on average 82 degrees year round. This year the island was devastated by Hurricanes Irma and Maria. They are experiencing a humanitarian crisis with much of the island still without power, roads and little access to clean water and food.

The question shouldn’t be what we ought to do, but what we can do.

Rory Stewart

You can help! Aid 4 Puerto Rico is a website created to help quicklt gain access to Non Profit Organizations helping provide care and rebuild Puerto Rico. Please consider helping, in what way you can to aid these island people.
